About me

Sita G - author and singer of rock and lyrical guitar songs. Lyrics filled with inspiring and uplifting spirit lines, often resonates with instructions and revelations of the great spiritual teachers of mankind.

"I want to fill every minute of your songs, because they are wonderful and their force is not put into words." (Ksenia)

"I do not like the genre, like turn off, but stopped me goosebumps despite the simplicity of the words." (Pelagia)

"Sita, you are not a doer of you - the event." (Sergey Smolensk)
